Rock Paper Scissors Simulator

  • Select either the rock, paper, or scissors image to start.
  • Click on the gray box area to place it down. Place as many as you want!
  • Do this with the other two images left. Have at least two down!
  • Right click an image to remove it.
  • Adjust the game speed slider to either be slow (1) to fast (10)
  • Toggle on or off the avoidance AI behavior (rocks avoid scissors, etc.)
  • Click play to start the action!
  • Pause the game if you want to.
  • Click on clear to remove everything and start over.
